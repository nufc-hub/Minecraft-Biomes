Plains

Description

In Minecraft, the "Plains" biome is one of the most common biomes in the game and is characterized by its flat terrain and grassy landscape.
This biome typically consists of large expanses of grassy fields, with an abundance of flowers and occasionally some scattered trees.

Ecology

The weather in the Plains biome is generally sunny and mild, which makes it a good place to set up a base camp or build a farm. The grass in
the biome is tall and yellow, and there are also many flowers that can be found here.

The Plains biome is also home to many different types of mobs, such as cows, pigs, sheep, and chickens, which can be farmed for their
resources. The biome also spawns horses, which can be tamed and ridden.

Mobs

The Plains biome in Minecraft is home to a variety of mobs. Cows can be found grazing in the grassy fields of the Plains biome. They can be milked for milk and their meat can be used for food. These mobs are passive and will not attack the player unless hit first.

Pigs can also be found roaming the Plains biome. They drop pork when killed and can be ridden with a saddle. They are also passive mobs and will not attack the player unless hit first.

Sheep can be found grazing in the grassy fields of the Plains biome. They can be sheared for wool and their meat can be used for food. Like the other mobs, sheep are passive and will not attack the player unless hit first.
